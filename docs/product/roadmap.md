# Product Roadmap

This page is a reader-facing roadmap index. It does not replace sprint plans,
runtime maturity, or external gate evidence.

## Current Direction

- Keep the local analyst and daemon workflows easy to start.
- Keep SDK/platform integration behind the experimental maturity label.
- Keep compatibility surfaces shrinking through documented sunset gates.
- Keep Kimi SA demo material separate from product setup docs.
