# Research Copilot Sample Prompts

## Chinese

结合公司最新年报、路演材料、市场趋势和当前组合持仓，分析其盈利质量、行业景气度与组合风险。所有数字必须经过工具核验，列出三项需要提交投资委员会讨论的问题。

## English

Analyze the company's earnings quality, industry momentum, and portfolio impact using the uploaded annual report, earnings presentation, market data, and current holdings. Validate every material number and identify three issues for the next investment committee meeting.
